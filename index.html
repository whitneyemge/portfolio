About Me

I am a Pacific Northwest native with an interest in the intersection of ethnography, participatory GIS, and international conservation. I currently work as a GIS Analyst for the Washington State Department of Archaeology and Historic Preservation, where I enjoy a variety of cartographic and data QC assignments. When I am not at work, school, or sitting in traffic between the two, I enjoy snowboarding, travelling, and making up songs about my three cats.
